# ✈️ Triploom – Travel Booking App UI

A complete mobile UI design for **Triploom**, a travel booking app that lets users discover destinations, book trips, and connect with fellow travelers. Designed with a bold, dark-themed aesthetic and vibrant destination imagery.

## 📱 Overview

Triploom guides users through a full booking journey — from onboarding and authentication, to browsing destinations, viewing detailed trip info, and completing a booking with payment confirmation.

## 📂 Screens Included

1. **Splash / Onboarding Screen** — App intro with tagline and "Get Started" CTA
2. **Sign Up Screen** — Create account with Full Name, Email/Phone, Password, and social sign-up options
3. **Sign In Screen** — Welcome back login screen with email/password and social sign-in
4. **Destination Detail Screens** — Switzerland, Sri Lanka, and Germany pages with ratings, descriptions, stay options, and pricing
5. **Booking Confirmation Screen** — Trip summary form (Destination, Dates, Travelers, Price Breakdown) with "Confirm & Pay"
6. **Success Screen** — Booking confirmation toast/message
7. **Home / Explore Screen** — Search bar, category icons (Flights, Hotel, Foods, Events), and a scrollable destination grid (Switzerland, Canada, Sri Lanka, Australia, Norway, Maldives, India, Germany, UK, America, Finland, Thailand)

## 🎨 Design Highlights

- **Theme:** Dark mode UI with vibrant travel photography
- **Primary Accent Color:** Sky Blue (`#3EC1E8` approx.)
- **Success Color:** Green (for confirmation states)
- **Rating badges**, **category chips**, and **stay-option pill tags** for quick scanning
- Full-bleed destination images with overlaid text and glassmorphism-style detail cards
